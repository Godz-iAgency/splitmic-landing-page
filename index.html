    How It Works

    Real stories.
    Real results.

    01
    Festival Needs a Band

    A festival is looking for an opening act. Instead of posting on Facebook and waiting, they log into Split Mic. They filter by genre and location. They see which bands are ready to book. They reach out with one click. Done.

    02
    Venue Posts an Open Mic

    A venue has an open slot on Thursday night. They post it on Split Mic in 60 seconds. Every musician in Austin sees it right away. Sign-ups come in. The night fills up. No phone calls. No emails back and forth.

    03
    Tourist Finds a Show

    Someone is visiting Austin from New York. They open Split Mic and see every live show happening tonight. They tap the one they like. The app calls them an Uber and shows them the route on Google Maps. They arrive. They have the time of their life.

    04
    Fan Tips Their Favorite Band

    A fan watches an amazing set. They open Split Mic and go straight to the band's profile. They tap to send a tip by credit card. The money goes directly to the band. No jar. No Venmo. Just love made easy.

    The Roadmap

    We are building this
    in three steps.

    Phase 1
    Launching First
    The Network

    Venues, performers, talent buyers, record labels, festivals, and tourists all on one platform. Post shows. Find gigs. Discover live music. Book shows. Connect directly with one click. Also fully connected to Uber and Google Maps so fans always know how to get there.

    Phase 2
    Coming Next
    Direct Tipping

    Fans and guests can go directly to a band's profile inside the app and send a tip via credit card or any online payment. Money goes straight to the artist. No middleman. More money in the pockets of Austin musicians.

    Phase 3
    The Big Vision
    Full Logistics Integration

    When you book a show through Split Mic, you can also book your backline, rent your instruments, and reserve a rehearsal studio, all in the same app. We will be partnering with a local Austin backline company, instrument rental service, and rehearsal studio to create a seamless experience for the Austin music network. No other platform in the world does this.
